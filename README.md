HerSpace 💖

Your Soft AI Wellness Companion for Women

HerSpace is a calming AI-powered wellness web app designed especially for women 🌸
It offers emotional support, wellness guidance, AI recipe suggestions, food scanning, motivation boosts, breathing exercises, yoga-based relief, and gentle self-care experiences for temporary relaxation and mind freshening.

Whether you’re stressed, tired, overwhelmed, emotionally drained, dealing with body pain, or simply looking for a peaceful space online — HerSpace is made to feel soft, supportive, and comforting.
